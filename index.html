What We Do!

GISJohn is dedicated to providing Africans with the necessary Geospatial Information & Services. Currently we are only working on projects for South Africa, but, expect exapnsion soon, our goal is to make complex census data simplified, but, not falsified! We want to make it easier for everyone to understand the demographics of the mother continent, we believe everyone should have easy access to census data without reading complex reports. We pride ourselves on delivering processed and graphically represented Census data, ESRI Shapefiles, Python Applications, GIS Software Tutorials.
