What We Do!

GISJohn is dedicated to providing South Africans with the necessary Geospatial Information & Services. Currently our goal is to make complex census data simplified, but, not falsified! We want to make it easier for everyone to understand the demographics of this fine country, we believe everyone should have easy access to census data without reading complex reports. We pride ourselves on delivering processed and graphically represented Census data, ESRI Shapefiles, Python Applications, GIS Software Tutorials.
